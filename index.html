Muddled Words

Rules

You're trying to get your team to guess the phrase on the card through various methods. For each attempt, you have 30 seconds to try to get your team to guess the answer using the restrictions required for the attempt. If your team is unable to guess the answer after all 3 rounds, the other team has one shot to steal and get 3 points.
Attempt 1 - 3pts - Act it out
Attempt 2 - 2pts - Draw it out
Attempt 3 - 1pt - Talk it out

Muddled Words is a game by Steven Ward
